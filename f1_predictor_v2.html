Key Findings
Finding 1

All three ML models beat the baseline (grid position only). Linear Regression achieved the best cross-validated MAE of 3.28 positions — an 11% improvement over simply predicting grid = finish.

Finding 2

Across all races, adding weather features had minimal impact. Grid position and qualifying pace dominate as predictors under normal dry conditions.

★ Star Finding

When isolated to wet races only, the weather-aware model achieved MAE of 2.87 vs a baseline of 3.68 — a 22% improvement. Weather is most powerful when conditions are most extreme.
